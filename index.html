Location

The country of Hornet County is right in the center of Australia and New Zealand. This gives us a chance to include parts from all cultures in Australia and New Zealand!

In Hornet County, and especially Hendrix Harbor, there are heaps of little touches from the American cultures. This pushes some better roleplay experiences in all of our games!

Our roleplay experience being immersive is one of the things we pride most. With realistic buildings, vehicles, stores, phones, and even some pretty cool guns!
